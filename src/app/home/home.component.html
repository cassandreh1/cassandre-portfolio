About me

   Bonjour le Monde! I’m Cassandre Henriquez, born in Brooklyn, and raised in Haiti. Following the devastating 2010 earthquake in Haiti, my family relocated to Wellington, Palm Beach, FL where I graduated high school. Upon graduation I initially enrolled at Palm Beach State College later transferring to Florida Atlantic University where I majored in and graduated with a degree in Computer Science. Initially, I was unsure which path I wanted to follow as a CS major (vast IT universe)! It wasn’t until I took a web development class that I found my niche. I enjoy bringing a website/web application to life and working on creative websites that push my CSS skills.

   I have spent the past three years as a developer in different capacities in FSI (Financial Services and Insurance) industry. This portfolio showcases my skills and upskill (forever a developer’s journey). Check it out and reach out!
